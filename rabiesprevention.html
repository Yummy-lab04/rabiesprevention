Myth
"Only dogs that foam at the mouth have rabies."

FACT: Animals can have "dumb rabies," where they appear quiet, lethargic, or paralyzed. Do not approach stray animals acting strangely.

Myth
"I can treat the bite with garlic or vinegar."

FACT: Folk remedies do NOT kill the virus. Only proper medical Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP) works.

Myth
"Puppies don't need vaccination."

FACT: Puppies can be infected and transmit the virus. They need their first shot as early as 3 months.
